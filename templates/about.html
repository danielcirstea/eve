About

1. General information

The E-learning Virtual Environment was made by Daniel Cirstea using the latest and most efficient web technologies. It was built on the Python Programming Language and the Django framework. This way, was ensured that the app is a highly secure one and does its job in a fast and simple way. It was also made from a student perspective and provides an attractive design.

2. Facts about EVE

1. The development process took approximately 11 weeks.
2. It rivals Moodle and as well as other learning platforms because of its security.
3. It is the first e-learning platform developed with Python.
4. The idea of building something like this I had it since the summer of 2017.
5. EVE is actually the first web application I ever made from scratch.

Special thanks to my teacher, Conf. Univ. Dr. Doru Popescu Anastasiu, University of Pitești, Romania.
